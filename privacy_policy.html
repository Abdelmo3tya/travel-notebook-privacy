Privacy Policy – Travel Notebook App

Last updated: October 31, 2025

Abdelmo3ty (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Travel Notebook mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

The Travel Notebook App does not collect or store any personal information on external servers. All user data — including travel destinations, expenses, to-do lists, and currency conversions — are stored locally on your device.

2. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is saved offline using secure local storage (SQLite or SharedPreferences).
  • No data is shared, uploaded, or transmitted to us or any third-party servers.
  • You have full control over your data at all times.

3. Permissions

The app may require access to:

  • Storage: To save and retrieve your travel data locally.
  • Internet (optional): Only for live currency exchange rate updates.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App is safe for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we’ll update the “Last updated” date above. You’re encouraged to review this policy occasionally for any changes.
